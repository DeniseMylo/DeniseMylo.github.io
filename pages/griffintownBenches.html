Made of Wood and steel, an
homage to Griffintown's
history of industrial factories.

With the strong belief that public space is a primary
manifestation of a sense of collection, designer Civiliti
considers that the shape we give our public spaces shapes
as well the idea of community and inscribes it physically
in the landscape.

The angular geometric shape
of the bench is inspired by the
architecture of the
neighbourhood and the shape
of the Peel Basin.
